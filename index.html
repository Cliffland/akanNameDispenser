Akan Name Dispenser.

Akan name Dispenser is a Web Application that takes users birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their akan name.
The Akan are a meta-ethnicity living primarily in Ghana. Akan Language (a.k.a Twi/Fante) are a group of dialects within the Central Tano branch of the Potou Tano Subfamily of the Niger-Congo Family. The Akan subgroups all have cultural attributes in common; mostly notably the tracing of matrilineal descent, inheritance of property, and succession to high political office.
The Akan people frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born.
