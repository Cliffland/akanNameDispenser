Akan Name Generator.

Akan name generator is a Web Application that takes users birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their akan name.
The Akan are a meta-ethnicity living primarily in Ghana. Then Akan Language (a.k.a Twi/Fante) are a group of dialects within the Central Tano branch of the Potou Tano Subfamily of the Niger-Congo Family. The Akan subgroups all have cultural attributes in common; mostly notably the tracing of matrilineal descent, inheritance of property, and succession to high political office.
